Places to Visit

Melbourne is a city filled with iconic landmarks like Federation Square, cultural gems such as the National Gallery of Victoria, and diverse neighborhoods. Visitors can explore historic markets, lush green spaces, and unique street art-adorned laneways.

Weather

Melbourne experiences four seasons, with warm summers, mild autumns, cool winters, and blooming springs, offering a wide range of weather experiences throughout the year.

Pedestrian Culture

Melbourne is renowned for its walker-friendly environment, featuring wide sidewalks, extensive laneways, dedicated bike lanes, and pedestrian zones. The city hosts pedestrian-friendly events, making it a delightful place for exploration on foot or by bike.
