Frequently Asked Questions

What is this dashboard used for?

Overall, the purpose of this software is to assist tourists in determining travel plans and travel times from multiple aspects and dimensions. In detail, this interface aims to facilitate tourists' travel in terms of human traffic, food, accommodation, weather, and amenities.

What platform should be used?

It is currently accessed through a web browser, it's advisable to continue using a web-based platform to maintain accessibility and convenience for users.

What are the data sources?

The main data sources for this dashboard can be found in City of Melbourne.

The weather data sources can be found in OpenWeather.

How is the combination of Shiny and Tableau?

The combination of Shiny and Tableau in our project involves the use of a Tableau dashboard for pedestrain density, while the other portion leverages Shiny along with Leaflet for interactive data visualization and analysis.
